    • The Text Reader Assistant

    If you are someone like me who doesn't like to waste time on a boring thing like reading essays or if you like to multitask, then this app is for you.
    You can simply past your essay or any text inside the text box and press play So the handsome JavaScript take care of reading the text for you.

    You can also easily change the speed of reading to your choice and can pause it at any place so later by pressing play it will continue from where you stopped.
